About OptiCrop

A smarter way to turn soil and climate data into profitable crop decisions.

What OptiCrop does

OptiCrop uses machine learning to recommend the most suitable crop based on environmental and soil parameters such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall.

Why it matters

The system helps farmers improve yield, reduce guesswork, and make more sustainable decisions for modern agriculture.
